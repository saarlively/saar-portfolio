MVP Product Experience & UI

Early concepts → wireframes → high‑fidelity prototypes for an MVP and fundraising story. Designed core flows and visual system for a trust‑first assistant that uses your memories, learning history, and trusted network to help you decide what to do next.

UX/UI Designer Visual Designer UX Researcher Tools: Figma
My role

I owned the end‑to‑end product experience — from early exploration and flow definition to UI, component system, and high‑fidelity prototypes used for MVP planning and fundraising.

  • Defined core journeys (Home, Chat, Results, Onboarding, Sources/Filters)
  • Designed the UI system (type, spacing, colors, components)
  • Built click‑through prototypes for stakeholder reviews and fundraising
Design approach
  • Started with quick sketches and low‑fidelity flows to align on the product promise
  • Iterated into wireframes to validate navigation, mental models, and information hierarchy
  • Moved into a consistent visual system to make the experience feel trustworthy and calm
  • Used prototypes to pressure‑test key moments: “show your work”, sources, and confidence
